How to play

Evil geese are depleting the natural resources rapidly. You're a cutting edge robot tasked with the responsibility of controlling the geese population. You score 10 points per goose eliminated. Your default weapon is the pistol. Rockets unlocks with 100 points. Laser unlocks with 500 points.
